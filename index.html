Books of 2024

Reading a book is one of my favorite ways to pass time. I enjoy books about time travel, parallel realities, space travel, history, thrillers and mysteries. Getting drawn in to a fictional new world makes the stress of the world around me disappear. It's also fun to learn about topics I'm not familiar with. These are my favorite reads from 2024!

  • The Gone World is a mind bending science fiction thriller blending time travel, quantum theory, and noir-style detective work. The story follows Shannon Moss, a NCIS agent investigating gruesome murders connected to deep space exploration.
  • Recursion is a gripping science fiction thriller that dives into the nature of memory, identity, and reality. Neuroscientist Helena Smith develops a technology to preserve memories, but inadvertently spawns a phenomenon called False Memory Syndrome where people experience alternate lives.
  • The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo a captivating novel about fame, love, and identity. Reclusive Hollywood icon Evelyn Hugo chooses unknown journalist Monique Grant to write her life story, unveiling scandal and her seven marriages.
  • Zero Days is a fast paced thriller following Jack, a penetration tester, whose life is upended when she finds her husband brutally murdered and she becomes the prime suspect.
  • Eleanor Oliphant is Completely Fine is a heartwarming novel about loneliness, healing, and human connection. Eleanor, a socially awkard woman leading an isolated life with a traumatic past, develops an unexpected friendship with Raymond that changes her life.

Top 5 TV Shows

These are the shows I could rewatch over and over again. Whether they get put on as background noise, or actively watched. I enjoy shows that are packed with drama, have a lot of twists and turns, and are layered with comedy.

  • Charmed follows The Charmed Ones, the most-powerful, good witches of all time, who use their power to protect innocent lives from demons and warlocks. Each sister possesses unique magical powers that grow and evolve, while they attempt to maintain normal lives.
  • One Tree Hill is set in North Carolina, and initially follows the lives of two half-brothers, Lucas and Nathan Scott, who compete for positions on their school's basketball team and the drama that ensues from the brothers' respective romantic relationships.
  • Malcolm in the Middle revolves around Malcolm, who's revealed in the first episode to be a genius with an IQ of 165, which places him in a class for gifted students. He is the third-born child in a comically dysfunctional family of four boys, of Lois and Hal.
  • How to Get Away with Murder revolves around Annalise Keating, a defense attorney and law professor at a prestigious Philadelphia university, who, along with five of her students, becomes involved in a complex murder plot.
  • Gossip Girl follows a group of privileged teenagers who attend a prestigious high school in the Upper East Side of New York City. Their private lives are constantly commented upon by an unknown blogger under the pseudonym "Gossip Girl".

Things to Learn About

There are so many interesting things to learn about in the world, and not enough time to learn them all! There is a whole range of topics that I like to learn about, but the topics I'm most interested in are listed below. I like to learn by reading books, watching videos and documentaries, or by first-hand practice.

Cosmology

Stars, planets, galaxies, black holes, and the greatest mystery of all, the nature of the Universe. I was always interested in the night sky as a kid, but it wasn't until I took an Astronomy class in college that I really started to learn about space. My favorite astronomical object is Hoag's Object.

Knitting

Knitting is a time consuming hobby. I learned how to knit by following along with video tutorials. I only know basic stiches right now, but one day I want to make a big blanket with a beautiful pattern on it. Or maybe even knit myself a sweater. There are so many fun colors of yarn to choose from!

Cooking

My partner and I recently just bought a house. Now that we have our own kitchen, an exploration into cooking has begun. I try to follow along with recipe books or blogs, but it's fun to tweak the recipes with new flavors. My current favorite dish to make is Green Chile Chicken Enchiladas.

Golf

I played golf in high school, but wasn't any good at it and didn't take it seriously. Now that I'm older and have a better appreciation for the sport, I enjoy getting out to play more. I'm learning how to have a consistent swing, proper stance and alignment, how to read the greens, and the rules of the game.

Fishing

Fishing is one of the most relaxing ways to spend a day, with the peaceful outdoors and calm water. I didn't get into fishing until pretty recently, so I'm still learning about which fishing rod to use, which lures to use and which hook size, line weights, and more, all depending on which type of fish you're trying to catch.

Cars

Car maintenance is the next thing I want to start learning about. So far I only know how to change my headlights, but one day I hope to know how to change a flat tire, change the oil, and replace a battery.
